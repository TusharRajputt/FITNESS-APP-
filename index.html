Fitness Tracker

User Personas

Persona 1: Emily, The College Student

Background: Emily is a 20-year-old college student majoring in Biology. She leads a sedentary lifestyle due to long study hours and irregular eating habits.

Motivations: Improve overall fitness to combat stress and boost energy levels. Maintain a healthy weight and physique.

Goals: Start with basic exercises like walking and stretching. Track progress easily to stay motivated.

Frustrations: Lack of time due to academic commitments. Confusion about where to start and how to progress effectively.

Persona 2: Mike, The Busy Professional

Background: Mike is a 32-year-old lawyer with a demanding job that requires long hours at a desk. He has neglected fitness due to work pressures.

Motivations: Reduce stress and improve mental clarity. Enhance overall health and well-being.

Goals: Incorporate short, effective workouts into his daily routine. Monitor progress to stay accountable.

Frustrations: Limited time for gym visits or structured exercise programs. Difficulty finding workouts that fit his schedule and fitness level.

Persona 3: Lisa, The Stay-at-Home Parent

Background: Lisa is a 35-year-old stay-at-home parent with two young children. She used to be active but finds it challenging to prioritize fitness while managing household responsibilities.

Motivations: Increase energy levels and improve mood. Set a positive example for her children.

Goals: Find quick and manageable workouts she can do at home. Connect with a supportive community for motivation.

Frustrations: Limited access to childcare for gym visits. Need for simple, family-friendly fitness solutions.
